Before the Fame

Before becoming a professional cyclist with Jumbo-Visma, Jonas rode for a few smaller domecstic pro teams while working 6-12 AM at a fish factory near to where he lived.

2021

2021 was the first time Jonas made it to the Tour de France with Jumbo-Visma. After the teams leader Primoz-roglic suffered a bad crash, Jonas was put into the position of leader. He took the most of this opportunity and finished second overall to Tadej pogacar.

2022

2022 was a big year for Jonas and Jumbo-Visma. Together they won the 2022 addition of the Tour de France. Before the tour, Jonas had many victories in smaller stage races asserting his dominance over his soon to be competition in the Tour de France.

2023

In 2023 Jonas won the Tour for a second year in a row by an even larger margin than his first victory. During the finall TT of the race Jonas was able to have one of the best rides of his life and put largley significant amounts of time into his closest competitiors. Thanks to this sucessful TT and the help of his team, Jonas was able to defeat his beggest rival, Tadej Pogacar, once again.

2024

Before Jonas could make it to the Tour de France he suffered a terrible crash along with many other competitiors during an early season stage race. Jonas was taken to the hospital and found to have a broken collarbone and a few broken ribs. His participation and from for the Tour is in question.
